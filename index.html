Welcome to Cake Blossom

At Cake Blossom, every cake is a story waiting to be told. From birthdays to weddings, and every little celebration in between — we craft cakes that bring joy, beauty, and delicious memories.

Our mission is simple: to spread happiness through the magic of baking. We believe a cake is not just dessert — it’s a heartfelt expression, a center of celebration, and a forever memory.

Our Vision

To become the go-to name for customized celebration cakes in the city — known for creativity, quality, and emotional connection.

Meet the Heart Behind the Whisk

Benish Iram, a 22-year-old passionate baker, started Cake Blossom with a dream and a spatula. From baking in her tiny kitchen to serving hundreds of happy customers, she believes that love and art can be felt in every bite.

Her goal? To make people feel seen, celebrated, and loved — one handcrafted cake at a time. Every flower you see on a cake, every color chosen, every message written — is done with intention and care.
